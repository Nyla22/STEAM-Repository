What is ClubHub?

  • a place for club officers to advertise their clubs
  • a place for students to see what clubs they can join
  • a place for club members to see when their meetings are
  • a place for studnets to see what clubs they can donate to

Report A Bug/Make A Suggestion

If you submit a bug or make a suggesiton, the ClubHub team will do our best to take a look at it. However, ClubHub is an open source project. If someone has a feature they'd really like to make happen, we encourage anyone to take a look at our Github repositroy, fork it, and upload their suggested code to a branch. For more information, contact us (using the form above.)
